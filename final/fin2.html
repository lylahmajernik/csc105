Here is a list of my top 10 Cat care tips:

Hover over each item

  1. Make sure to keep full glasses of water on the table for knocking over.
  2. Dry-food containers are a scam. Just leave the bag open and on the floor.
  3. Wet food is actually less expensive if you just do math bad.
  4. When your cat is trying to stand on your keyboard, please let them.
  5. Do not brush your cat, it is bad for them and they hate it.
  6. Before learning to walk, infants should learn how to open tin cans.
  7. The two best sources of water for a cat are a $150 automated water fountain, and the toilet.
  8. Cats love to cuddle, but only in the most uncomfortable place for you. Suffer or stay cold.
  9. Bird videos on Youtube are better than any movie.
  10. Make sure you buy lots of cat toys so your cat has something to ignore while they play with the random dust bunny on the floor.
