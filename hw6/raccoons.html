Let's Learn About Raccoons!

Raccoons are my favorite animal. Today, I'd like to share some fun facts with you!

Facts

Raccoons are mammals that can be found througout much of the world.

They are notorious for their black "mask" of fur around their eyes, and their big striped tails.

Raccoons have a lifespan of 2-3 years in the wild, but can live up to 20 years in captivity!

Diet

Raccoons eat a diet of: fruit, seeds, nuts, eggs, and plants.

They are excellent swimmers, and also enjoy hunting fish, frogs, and crayfish.

Perhaps most infamously, they are known for their love of trash. Raccoons are great scavengers!
